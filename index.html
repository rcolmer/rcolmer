Rachel Colmer

Rachel Colmer was born in Sheffield and spent her childhood in Yorkshire and Hertfordshire. Having originally trained as a nursery nurse, she worked in London and then emigrated to Canada where she worked as a nanny. After her return to the UK, she gained an MA (Ed) and became an adult educator and trainer. During this time she undertook counselling training and subsequently became a bereavement counsellor. She is a mother and grandmother and lives in East Anglia with her husband.

Do Rabbits Eat Marshmallows?

Sarah-Jane and Andrew, as sister and brother, have always had each other and have therefore been able to weather the difficulties of their lives. Tom however, has carried guilt and anger ever since he found out about his wife's illness. He tries hard to shut it all away but, when he meets his new neighbour Sarah-Jane, life takes an unexpected turn and he has at last to face his emotions and start the road to forgiveness. As his new life unfolds, life deals unexpected twists and turns and another devasting blow. 

Is Tom able to find a way through his pain and so hold his loved ones together...?
