My Debut Novel!

Do Rabbits Eat Marshmallows?

Sarah-Jane and Andrew, as sister and brother, have always had each other and have therefore been able to weather the difficulties of their lives. Tom, however, has carried guilt and anger ever since he found out about his wife's illness. He tries hard to shut it all away but, when he meets his new neighbour Sarah-Jane, life takes an unexpected turn and he has at last to face his emotions and start the road to forgiveness. As his new life unfolds, life deals unexpected twists and turns and another devasting blow.

Is Tom able to find a way through his pain and so hold his loved ones together...?

About Me

I was born in Sheffield to Welsh parents and my childhood was spent in Yorkshire, Hertfordshire and South Wales. Having left school at 16, I worked as a Community Service Volunteer in three different residential settings, working with children and adults. I went on to train as a nursery nurse and worked as a staff nursery nurse in a deprived area of London. Following this, I emigrated to Canada where I worked as a nanny, travelling extensively in North America. After my return to the UK, I gained an MA (Ed) and became an adult educator and trainer. During this time I undertook counselling training and subsequently became a bereavement counsellor. I am a mother and grandmother and live in East Anglia with my husband.
